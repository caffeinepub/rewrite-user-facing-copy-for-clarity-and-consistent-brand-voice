# Specification

## Summary
**Goal:** Consolidate Righteous Truths to Version 1 master foundation by verifying all critical features are functional and properly integrated.

**Planned changes:**
- Verify all four AI-powered creative tools (coloring page generator, affirmation creator, meme creator, DTF artwork generator) are functional and accessible from Creative Studio
- Ensure DTF gallery and store operations including upload, preview, purchase, and download functionality
- Verify admin dashboard displays all management features: upload controls, licensing approvals, royalty tracking, and analytics metrics
- Confirm licensing and royalty automation workflow including consent modal, licensing badges, and accurate backend calculations
- Verify Stripe embedded checkout links are functional with proper payment processing and purchase recording
- Ensure all legal pages (Privacy Policy, Terms of Service, Copyright Notice, Refund Policy) are complete and accessible
- Verify optimized branding consistency across all pages including logos, colors, typography, and AnimatedButton components
- Confirm production checklist validates critical configurations: Stripe setup, SEO metadata, environment settings, and admin permissions
- Ensure embeddable widgets function properly and adapt to iframe contexts

**User-visible outcome:** Users can access a fully functional creative platform with working AI tools, complete DTF marketplace with purchasing capability, proper licensing workflows, consistent branding throughout, and all legal documentation in place.
